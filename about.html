About Us

Welcome to HomeDoc, where healthcare meets convenience and compassion. At HomeDoc, we believe in redefining healthcare delivery, making it accessible, patient-centric, and personalized.

Our mission at HomeDoc is to revolutionize the healthcare experience by bridging the gap between patients and medical professionals. We aim to provide on-demand healthcare services, bringing expert medical care directly to your doorstep.

HomeDoc is more than just a healthcare platform; we are a team of dedicated professionals committed to making a positive impact on the lives of individuals and communities. Our team comprises experienced healthcare professionals, tech enthusiasts, and innovators passionate about transforming the way healthcare is delivered.

The Significance of the HULT Prize Competition

Winning 2nd place at the Hult Prize business competition is a tremendous achievement for us. It not only validates the innovative nature of our business idea but also demonstrates our team's ability to develop impactful solutions in Bangladesh. This recognition provides us with valuable exposure, credibility, and access to resources that will accelerate our journey towards making a real difference in the healthcare industry of Bangladesh. We are grateful for the opportunity and excited to leverage this success to further our mission of revolutionizing healthcare delivery.
